Barcelona Sign Joan García from Espanyol in Surprise Move

Barcelona Sign Joan García from Espanyol in Surprise Move

FC Barcelona has officially reached an agreement with local rivals RCD Espanyol to sign goalkeeper Joan García. The deal, reportedly worth €20 million, marks a strategic investment in the club’s future between the posts.

From Cornellà to Camp Nou

Born in Sabadell and developed at Espanyol’s academy, Joan García has quickly earned a reputation as one of Spain’s most promising young goalkeepers. At just 24, his agility, shot-stopping ability, and calm presence under pressure have made him a standout in La Liga.

A Long-Term Vision

With Marc-André ter Stegen entering the later years of his career, Barça has been searching for a successor who fits their footballing philosophy. Joan García checks all the boxes—technical skill, confidence with the ball at his feet, and strong reflexes. The move signals Barcelona’s commitment to planning for the future, while maintaining a competitive edge now.

Crossing the Divide

Transfers between Barça and Espanyol are rare—and always headline-grabbing. Joan García’s move is no different. Though the rivalry between the two clubs runs deep, García’s professionalism and potential made him too good to pass up.
