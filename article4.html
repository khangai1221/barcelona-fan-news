Barca announced they will go to Asia for the first time in 6 years

FC Barcelona has officially announced plans to return to Asia for a pre-season tour—their first visit to the continent in six years. The tour will take place this summer, with matches and fan events scheduled across key cities in Japan, South Korea, and China.

A Global Club Reconnecting with Asia

It’s been six years since Barça last played in Asia, and the return has been highly anticipated. The tour aims to strengthen the club’s global presence and reconnect with millions of passionate fans across the region. Barcelona will bring their full first-team squad, including stars like Robert Lewandowski, Pedri, and João Neves.

Building Momentum On and Off the Pitch

Beyond the friendly matches, the tour will include training sessions open to fans, youth clinics, and commercial events with major global partners. For head coach Xavi, it’s also a valuable opportunity to test the squad ahead of a demanding season.

Excitement Across the Region

“We're thrilled to be returning to Asia,” said a club spokesperson. “Barça is more than a club, and our fans in Asia have always shown incredible loyalty and love. We can’t wait to see them again.”
