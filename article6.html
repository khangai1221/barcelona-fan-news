Barça’s Pre-Season to Kick Off on July 13 for the First Time

FC Barcelona has confirmed that the 2025/26 pre-season will officially begin on July 13, marking the earliest start in recent club history. Players will return to Ciutat Esportiva Joan Gamper for medical tests and the first training session under Xavi’s leadership.

Early Start, Big Ambitions

With a packed calendar ahead—including the return to Asia and a competitive league season—Barça is starting early to maximize preparation time. The squad will use the extra weeks to build fitness, integrate new signings like João Neves and Joan García, and fine-tune tactics.

Youth and Experience Blend

Several Barça Atlètic players are expected to be called up for the first phase of training, giving them a chance to impress the coaching staff. Meanwhile, senior stars will gradually return from international duty.

Eyes on a Fresh Start

Fans can expect high-intensity sessions, behind-the-scenes content, and early signs of the team’s evolution. With fresh energy and renewed focus, July 13 marks the beginning of a crucial chapter for FC Barcelona.
