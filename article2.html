New signing: João Neves joins Barça

FC Barcelona has completed the signing of Portuguese midfield sensation João Neves from Benfica. The deal is reportedly worth €60 million, bringing one of Europe’s brightest young talents to the Camp Nou.

Rising Star from Benfica

At just 20 years old, João Neves has already made a name for himself as one of the most dynamic midfielders in Europe. Known for his composure on the ball, tireless work rate, and tactical intelligence, he played a key role in Benfica’s domestic success and caught the eye of top clubs across the continent.

A Midfield Reinforced

Barcelona’s midfield has long been the heartbeat of the team, from the days of Xavi and Iniesta to the rise of Pedri and Gavi. With the arrival of João Neves, Barça adds depth, energy, and future leadership to its engine room. His versatility allows him to play as a defensive midfielder or a box-to-box playmaker—just the kind of profile Xavi has been seeking.

Commitment to the Future

This signing is more than just a transfer—it's a statement. Barcelona is investing in youth, vision, and long-term stability. João Neves embodies the club’s philosophy: talent, intelligence, and passion for the game.
