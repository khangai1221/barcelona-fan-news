Inside look at Barça’s training session

At Ciutat Esportiva Joan Gamper, the atmosphere is a mix of intensity and optimism. As the sun rises over Sant Joan Despí, FC Barcelona’s players are already hard at work—laying the foundation for the coming season.

A High-Tempo Start

The session began with short-passing drills under the sharp eyes of Xavi and his coaching staff. Precision and movement were the focus. “Think fast, move faster,” shouted one assistant, as the midfield trio of Pedri, João Neves, and Gavi showed off their chemistry in tight spaces.

Fitness, Then Finesse

After a high-intensity rondo, the players transitioned into tactical work. Defensive lines were drilled repeatedly—especially pressing triggers and transitions. Ter Stegen commanded from the back, while Jules Koundé and Ronald Araújo communicated constantly, marshalling the shape. Meanwhile, the new arrivals—including João Neves and Joan García—continued to integrate into the squad. Neves, in particular, impressed with his energy and ability to break lines under pressure.

A Touch of Flair

Toward the end of the session, finishing drills brought smiles and cheers. Ansu Fati curled a beauty into the top corner. Lewandowski followed up with a no-look volley that had teammates applauding. Even the goalkeepers joined in the fun, showing off their reflexes in a series of one-on-ones.
